
-- Diplomancer -------------------------------------------------

An addon for World of Warcraft to automatically set your watched
faction based on your current location.

  http://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/info9643-Diplomancer

-- Localization: -----------------------------------------------

Diplomancer currently only works in English locales.

If you play in another locale and would like to know how you
can help make Diplomancer work for that locale, please see:

  http://www.wowinterface.com/portal.php?&id=224&pageid=160
