
-- Diplomancer ---------------------------------------------------------

A World of Warcraft addon to automatically set your watched faction
based on your location.

  http://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/info9643-Diplomancer.html

-- Localization: -------------------------------------------------------

Diplomancer currently only works in English clients.

If you play in another locale and would like to know how you can help
make Diplomancer work for that locale, please see:

  http://www.wowinterface.com/portal.php?&id=224&pageid=160
